Short Bio

I am a second-year PhD student at the Department of Computer Graphics and Interaction, Czech Technical University in Prague supervised by prof. Daniel Sýkora . I received my BSc and MSc degrees from Software Engineering at the same university. My primary research interest lies in computer graphics, image processing, computer vision, and deep learning; with focus on computer-assisted stylization and style transfer. In recent years, I have been working with Adobe Research on several publications and research-engineering projects; and I was granted the opportunity to join them as a research scientist intern in San Jose, California (2017) and then in Seattle, Washington (2018).
